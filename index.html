My Skills

Tinyfetch

A full web application & community for finding tiny homes and alternative living spaces. Users can create accounts, submit listings, leave ratings/reviews, explore extensive filters/search forms, direct messages, upload media and compare listings. Tinyfetch is fully integrated with payment processors, bookmarking, notification and email marketing systems, geolocation/maps, Google Site Kit and analytics. The technologies used in the creation of the site are HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, MySQL and WordPress. Visit Site.

Muskoka Framing

A framing company from Muskoka that needed a website to showcase their work and also generate more leads. The main objective they wanted with the website was to have strong search engine optimization. Since the site had been launched, their business had tripled and they are the first result in search engines. We were able to achieve this through strong on-page and organic SEO and there was no advertising needed. The technologies used in the site were HTML, CSS, Javascript and PHP. Visit Site.
