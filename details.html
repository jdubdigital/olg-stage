GRAND OPENING CELEBRATION

You and your guest are confirmed to celebrate the grand opening of the OLG Stage at Fallsview Casino with an exclusive pre-concert event and a live performance by the one and only Billy Joel!

Saturday, February 25, 2023

Pre-Concert Event Details:

LOCATION:
Avalon Theatre.
12:00PM–6:00PM:
Avalon Box Office open for ticket pick up.
5:30PM-7:30PM:
Cocktails, local culinary stations and grand opening presentations.

BILLY JOEL CONCERT:

LOCATION:
OLG Stage at Fallsview Casino.
6:30PM:
Doors Open.
8:00PM:
Billy Joel concert begins.

We will begin the celebration in our Avalon Theatre with local wines, expertly curated beverages, local food stations, passed hors d’oeuvres and presentations to celebrate our grand opening. Following the reception, enjoy a walk through our beautiful property to the new state-of-the-art theatre – the OLG Stage at Fallsview Casino, where you will enjoy an evening of world-class entertainment with Billy Joel.


If you have any questions for the event please do not hesitate to call us at 1.877.376.9722.

This invitation is non-transferrable.

Tickets can be picked up on the day of the event from 12pm until 6pm at the Avalon Theatre Box Office.

Please ensure you bring your Momentum card to pick up your tickets.

We look forward to celebrating with you!
